Global CDN Market –
Present Value and Future

October 16, 2020 by Rohma Azeem

Leave a Comment

What is CDN?

A content delivery network (CDN) is a system of servers or
workstations interlocked over the internet, that is located to users
across the globe to lessen the distance content travels. CDNs offer a
solution for proficiently and successfully handling the content of all
types.

Solutions include core services such as combination, management,
and distribution of content as well as the variation of value-added
functionality. Many CDN organizations can run on commercial off-
the-shelf hardware with virtual machines, thus decreasing costs and
refining infrastructure elasticity.

Content Delivery Network (CDN) Market Forecast:
(2020-2025)

CDN market value was $11.85 billion in 2019 and is expected to see
an increase during the forecast era and will extend to $24.70 billion
till 2025 rising at a CAGR of 13.4% between 2020 to 2025.
Exponentially increasing internet penetration in all the business
verticals along with the increasing deployment of this type of
delivery network for traffic handling has been encouraging market
growth across the world.

Solution:

The content delivery network market is divided into two parts i.e
media delivery, web performance optimization by a solution. The
media delivery division held the major share accounting for around
34% in 2019. CDN market value concerning media delivery solutions
was $4.06 billion in 2019 and a rise is estimated throughout the
forecast.

The market will reach $8.6 billion by 2025 rising at a CAGR of 13.66%
from 2020 to 2025.

End-User Industry:

Media and entertainment are the leading end-user industry of the
CDN market accounting for a 30% share of the market in 2019.
Users can also view and access the high definition video content
with web performance optimization and media delivery.

Social networking sites such as Facebook, Twitter, and others are
also one of the causes of increasing internet traffic. Around, 1500
million are registered users in social networking sites presently

Geography:

America is capable of holding the major share of 38% of the global
CDN market. It is predicted that it will reach $9.18 billion by 2025
rising at a CAGR of 13.2%. The market is examined to be driven by
enterprises choosing for CDN solutions. America’s CDN market value
was highly dependent on media and entertainment industry with
online gaming composed to witness the highest growth of CAGR
18.95% during 2020-2025. The largest growing CDN market in APAC
is predicted to grow at a CAGR of 18.46% from 2020 to 2025
projecting to $7.49 billion by 2025.

The market is composed to be driven by rising SMEs which decide
on cloud CDNs and P2P CDNs due to its cost efficiency and
flexibility. The penetration of huge scale e-commerce websites along
with the consumers’ preferences for online shopping is also pushing
for the service of CDNs by mobile companies which has enhanced
the market growth in this region.

The Effect of Covid-19:

Due to the covid-19 condition, there is an upsurge in work from
home opportunities, which can, directly and indirectly, lead to
greater media consumption. Remote classes and training plans also
add to improved IP traffic, which leads to more use of CDNs across
nations.

Conclusion:

All these technologies will dominate our education system as there
is a wide scope of such technologies in the future.
