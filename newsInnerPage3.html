“Ruined King” Launch
Confirmed in Early 2021

November 1, 2020 by Rohma Azeem

Leave a Comment

Introduction:

Riot forge is a popular American video game developer based in Los
Angeles. They have launched many games like League of legends,
Valorant, League of Legends: Wild Rift and Legends of Runeterra,
etc.

Ruined King is part of the Riot Forge company. It is an enterprise
that allows third-party developers to create games with the League
of Legends license. Airship Syndicate has developed this game. It is
the same studio that created Darksiders: Genesis.

Release of Trailer:

A trailer was released a few days back during the League of Legends
World Championship. In the trailer, it was shown that the group was
assembling in a tavern and then sailed towards mysterious land
which was controlled by the king.

The company has announced that League of Legends will be headed
to consoles with the release of the spin-off game Ruined King: A
League of Legends Story.

It is set in the world of Runeterra. Ruined King will be a single-player
RPG based game.

In this game, you will go on an adventurous journey through two
regions of Runeterra – Bilgewater and the Shadow Isles. A team of
beloved League of Legends champions like Miss Fortune, Illaoi,
Braum, Yasuo, Ahri, and Pyke is then formed.

Launch Date:

Riot hasn’t announced the actual release date but they have
released a statement in which they told that the game will launch in
early 2021. This game will also be available on Switch, PS4, Xbox,
and PC through Steam and the Epic Games Store.

“Ruined King” will be available on PS5 and the Xbox Series. In
addition to this, it will also allow the players to upgrade their
previous version for free.

Leanne Loombe, the Head of Riot Forge at Riot Games also
explained in a press release that “Ruined King is a great way to
expand the world of Runeterra for brand new players and also for
our dedicated League of Legends fans.”

Conclusion:

Riot forge has only dropped us some hints. It will soon tell us more
about the Ruined King: A League of Legends Story in December
2020.
